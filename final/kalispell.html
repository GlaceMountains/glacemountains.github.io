My Hometown: Kalispell, Montana

This nice town located in the Flathead Valley of Northwest Montana is one of the gateways to Glacier National Park. It is a great place to live as well as visit. It's population, as of 2017, was 23,212 people. That's about 8,000 less than Helena's 31,429 people(2017).
